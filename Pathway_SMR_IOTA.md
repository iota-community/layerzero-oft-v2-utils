# Pathway ShimmerEVM <-- wSMR --> IotaEVM

### On OFTAdapter (source chain of ShimmerEVM)

`export isForOFTAdapter=true && npx hardhat run scripts/set_enforced_options.ts --network shimmerEvmMainnet`
